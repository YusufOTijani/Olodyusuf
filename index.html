YUSUF OLATUNJI TIJANI

Rhodes University Grahamstown, SA Twitter ORCID LinkedIn ResearchGate GitHub

I am a postdoctoral research fellow in the Department of Mathematics, Rhodes University, Makhanda (Grahamstown), South Africa. My research interests lie in the design and implementation of numerical method, computational fluid dynamics, mathematical modelling of dynamical systems, fractional calculus and machine learning.

Before joining Rhodes University, I pursued my Doctoral studies at Nelson Mandela University, Port-Elizabeth, South Africa starting in 2019. My PhD research focused on developing and implementing a numerical scheme for the advection-reaction-diffusion equations. I developed a non-standard finite difference scheme for the preservation of a biofilm mathematical model, designed an accurate scheme for the Burgers-Huxley equation and singularly perturbed equations. Analysis such as travelling waves were numerically investigated.

I completed both my Bachelor's and Master's degrees in Mathematics at Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile-Ife, Nigeria, in 2013 and 2018, respectively. I did my compulsory one year youth service in Rivers-States, Nigeria, 2014-2015.
